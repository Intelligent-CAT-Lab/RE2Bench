The method `fahrenheit_to_celsius` applies the formula:
$$ \text{Celsius} = (\text{Temperature}_F - 32) \times \frac{5}{9} $$

Given the output (Celsius) is `26.666666666666668`, we need to solve for `self.temperature` (Temperature_F).

1.  **Reverse the Equation**:
    $$ \text{Temperature}_F = \text{Celsius} \times \frac{9}{5} + 32 $$

2.  **Substitute the Output value**:
    $$ \text{Temperature}_F = 26.666666666666668 \times 1.8 + 32 $$

3.  **Calculate**:
    *   $26.666...$ is equivalent to $26 + 2/3$ or $80/3$.
    *   $(80/3) \times (9/5) = (80 \times 3) / 5 = 240 / 5 = 48$.
    *   $48 + 32 = 80$.

So, `self.temperature` must be 80. The other fields in `self` (like `city`) do not affect the output, so generic valid values are used.
